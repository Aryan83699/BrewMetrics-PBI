## 📖 Overview

The **BrewMetrics Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of coffee shop operations through dynamic visualizations and KPI monitoring. It enables business users to track revenue growth, identify top-performing products, compare store performance, and uncover customer buying patterns.

The dashboard is built to support data-driven decision-making by transforming raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

---

## 📊 Business Questions Answered

✔ Which product categories generate the highest revenue?

✔ Which store location performs best?

✔ What are the peak sales hours throughout the week?

✔ How do weekday sales compare with weekend sales?

✔ Which products contribute most to overall revenue?

✔ What are the month-over-month performance trends?

---

Additional metrics include:

- Month-over-Month Growth
- Revenue Trends
- Product Performance
- Store Performance
- Hourly Sales Distribution
- Weekday vs Weekend Analysis

## 📊 Insights Generated

### Product Analysis
- Compare sales across multiple product categories.
- Identify best-selling products and revenue drivers.
- Track category contribution to overall sales.

### Store Analysis
- Compare performance between store locations.
- Monitor location-wise revenue growth.
- Identify top-performing branches.

### Customer Behavior
- Analyze purchasing patterns by hour and weekday.
- Discover peak business periods.
- Evaluate weekday versus weekend performance.

### Sales Trends
- Monitor daily sales performance.
- Track monthly growth trends.
- Identify seasonal fluctuations and anomalies.
